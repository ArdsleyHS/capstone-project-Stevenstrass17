Tom Brady

He needs no introduction. He is the undisputed GOAT. Tom Brady has won a whopping 7 super bowls. 6 with the New England patriots and 1 with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, and has potential for more in the coming years. He also currently is ranked number 2 all time in passing yards only behind Drew Brees, and is third on the all time passing TD list behind Peyton Manning and Brett Favre.
Tom Brady's Superbowl Wins:
  1. 2002, on Patriots vs Rams
  2. 2004, on Patriots vs Panthers
  3. 2005, on Patriots vs Eagles
  4. 2015, on Patriots vs Seahawks
  5. 2017, on Patriots vs Falcons
  6. 2019, on Patriots vs Rams
  7. 2021, on Bucaneers vs Cheifs
