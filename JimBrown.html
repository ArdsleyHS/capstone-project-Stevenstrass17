Jim Brown

The Cleveland browns legend, Jim Brown, played in the NFL from 1957-65. He has 12,312 total rushing yards and averaged 5.2 yards per carry(An NFL record) and has 126 touchdowns. During a time period in the NFL where defenses were designed around stopping the run game, he still averaged over 100 rushing yards per game, the only running back in NFL history to do so. He was an 8 time nfl rushing champion, a four time MVP and a hall of famer. .
