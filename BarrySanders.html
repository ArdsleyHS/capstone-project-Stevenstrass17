Barry Sanders

Playing on the Detroit Lions from 1989-98, Barry sanders was one of the most electrifying players to watch. He speed and agility were unrivaled at the time, and he is 4th on the NFL all time rushing yards lists, despite a relatively short career. He averaged 5 yards per carry, and has a career total of 109 touchdowns. He was the MVP twice and was elected to the pro bowl 10 times. .
